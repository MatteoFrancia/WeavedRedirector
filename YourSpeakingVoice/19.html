YOUR SPEAKING VOICE
SPEECH PROFILE INSTRUCTIONS
For the Speaker:
1. Before presenting a manual speech, arrange with your club’s vice president education to have a
member evaluate your voice according to the Speech Profile.
2°
When the evaluation is completed, draw a line connecting the marks on the profile. Any character-
istics rated four or less on the scale indicate possible voice challenges.
3°
Refer to the corresponding exercises in this manual for help in correcting your voice challenges.
For the Evaluator:
1. Identify each of the characteristics on the speech profile.
During the speaker’s presentation, rate
each characteristic on a scale from one to seven, one being negative and seven positive.
2°
Return the profile to the speaker at the conclusion of the presentation. You may wish to verbally
evaluate the speaker’s voice.
